SAPIRO and Seterra are both geography apps, but with different approaches. Here is an honest comparison.

## The approach

**Seterra.** Geography-specialized reference app. Interactive blank maps to identify countries, capitals, flags, rivers, mountains. "Find on the map" mode.

**SAPIRO.** General knowledge app where geography is one of four universes (with history, art, nature). Multiple-choice quiz with contextualized explanation.

## Geographic content

**Seterra.** Exhaustive and very detailed geography: countries, capitals, flags, but also regions, federal states, rivers, mountains, deserts, lakes.

**SAPIRO.** Coverage of the 197 countries with flags and capitals, plus thematic questions (erosion, economic geography). Less exhaustive on pure geography, but with context.

## Gamification

**Seterra.** Very score and time oriented. Very satisfying for geography fanatics.

**SAPIRO.** More moderate, focused on learning through explanation. See our article on [gamification of learning](/en/blog/gamification-education/).

## Free tier

**Seterra.** Free with ads. Premium (about $3/month) without ads.

**SAPIRO.** Free without ads, no data collection. See our article on [best ad-free educational apps](/en/blog/best-ad-free-educational-apps/).

## Content diversity

**Seterra.** Geography only.

**SAPIRO.** Geography, history, art, nature. 2,000+ questions total.

## Target audience

**Seterra.** Geography fanatics who want to master the world map in all its details.

**SAPIRO.** Broader audience, curious about general knowledge, who wants geography as one of the components.

## When to choose each

**Choose Seterra if** you want to specialize in pure geography (blank maps, federal states, rivers, mountains). World reference of the genre.

**Choose SAPIRO if** you want geography in a broader cultural context, with history, art and nature added. No ads, no data collection.

## Can they coexist?

Yes, they complement each other. Seterra for pure map mastery. SAPIRO for broader general knowledge with geography included.
